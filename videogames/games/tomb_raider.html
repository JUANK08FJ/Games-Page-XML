Tomb Raider

This game is an action-adventure game developed by Crystal Dynamics. Released on May 3, 2013, the game offers a thrilling combination of action, platforming, and puzzle-solving elements. Players navigate the perilous journey of Lara Croft as she evolves from an inexperienced adventurer to a skilled survivor. With its engaging gameplay and stunning visuals, Tomb Raider received critical acclaim, earning a Metacritic score of 86.
