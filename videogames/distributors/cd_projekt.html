CD PROJEKT

Founded on May 19, 1994, in Warsaw, Poland, CD PROJEKT has emerged as a powerhouse in the gaming industry, renowned for its commitment to storytelling and immersive gameplay experiences. With two dedicated game studios, the company has solidified its reputation as a premier developer and publisher.

Cyberpunk 2077
In the futuristic realm of "Cyberpunk 2077," CD PROJEKT once again showcased its storytelling prowess. The game thrusts players into a dystopian future, blending a gripping narrative with a visually stunning open world.
The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt
A magnum opus in the RPG genre, "The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt" captivated players with its mature storytelling, complex characters, and morally ambiguous choices.
