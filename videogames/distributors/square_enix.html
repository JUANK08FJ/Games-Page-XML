Square Enix

Founded on May 28, 1983, in Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan, Square Enix stands as a visionary force in the gaming industry, shaping the landscape with a rich legacy of captivating narratives and innovative gameplay. With 14 game studios under its banner, the company continues to be a driving force in delivering unique and memorable gaming experiences.

Tomb Raider
A trailblazer in the action-adventure genre, "Tomb Raider" is a testament to Square Enix's ability to reimagine and revitalize classic franchises. The game follows the intrepid Lara Croft on her archaeological exploits, combining immersive exploration with intense action sequences.
NieR: Automata
In the realm of Japanese action RPGs, "NieR: Automata" has emerged as a critical and commercial triumph. Square Enix's collaboration with PlatinumGames resulted in a narrative masterpiece, exploring existential themes through the lens of android protagonists..
