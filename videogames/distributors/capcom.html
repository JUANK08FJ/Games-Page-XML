CAPCOM

Founded on November 6, 1979, in Chuo Ward, Osaka, Japan, Capcom stands as a stalwart in the gaming industry. With a rich legacy spanning over four decades, the company has contributed significantly to the evolution of video games. Boasting an impressive array of 14 game studios, Capcom has been a driving force behind iconic titles that have left an indelible mark on gaming culture.

Marvel VS Capcom 2: New Age of Heroes
A masterpiece in the realm of fighting games, Marvel VS Capcom 2: New Age of Heroes showcases Capcom's prowess in delivering adrenaline-pumping, crossover combat. Released to critical acclaim, this title remains a testament to the company's ability to bring together diverse universes in an engaging and visually stunning manner.
Resident Evil 7: Biohazard
In the horror genre, Capcom's Resident Evil 7: Biohazard stands as a pinnacle of fear-inducing gameplay. With a return to its survival horror roots, this installment redefined the Resident Evil series, captivating players with its immersive narrative, spine-chilling atmosphere, and innovative gameplay mechanics.
