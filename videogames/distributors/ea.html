EA

Founded on May 28, 1982, in San Mateo, California, USA, Electronic Arts (EA) has been a driving force in the gaming industry for over three decades. With an extensive network of 18 game studios, EA has consistently delivered groundbreaking titles that have left an indelible mark on the gaming landscape.

FIFA 17
As a cornerstone of the sports gaming genre, "FIFA 17" stands as a testament to EA's commitment to realism and innovation. Offering a captivating blend of precision gameplay and lifelike graphics.
The Sims 4
In the realm of life simulation, "The Sims 4" is a showcase of EA's creativity and player-centric design. Allowing players to create and control virtual lives with unprecedented depth, this game has become a cultural phenomenon.
Mirror's Edge
Breaking new ground in the first-person action-adventure genre, "Mirror's Edge" exemplifies EA's willingness to explore unconventional gameplay mechanics and artistic styles. With its distinctive visual aesthetic and parkour-inspired movement.
