Warner Bros. Games

Founded on January 14, 2004, in Burbank, California, USA, Warner Bros. Games has been a prominent player in the gaming industry, contributing to the world of interactive entertainment with a diverse portfolio of games. With 10 game studios, the company has cultivated a reputation for delivering engaging experiences across various genres.

LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga
Bringing together two iconic franchises, "LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga" exemplifies Warner Bros. Games' ability to blend humor, nostalgia, and engaging gameplay. This title invites players to relive the epic Star Wars saga in a whimsical LEGO universe.
Batman: Arkham City
As a cornerstone of superhero gaming, "Batman: Arkham City" showcases Warner Bros. Games' prowess in translating iconic comic book characters into immersive, action-packed adventures.
Mortal Kombat 11
The "Mortal Kombat" series, under Warner Bros. Games, continues to push the boundaries of fighting games with "Mortal Kombat 11." Known for its visceral combat, cinematic presentation, and iconic characters
